Contabilita

Coffee: 

/day

GitHub: contabilita_rails

Website: Contabilita

This is a web-app developed with Rails. The pourpose of it is to help little transport companies with the accountability. By registering all the expences (invoices, tickets etc) which belong to companies, categories and vehicles, the user can see how much has been spent per year or per month for every category, company and vehicle.

Approach

I've first started by trying to understand how the accoutability of a company works. Then by talking to a potential user, I've tried to understand the critical points of how the costs should be calculated and which are those costs. Then I've sketched the neccessary views and the neccessary objects/class that were needed to make the app function. Then by researching and trial and error I've started developing the web-app.

Conclusions

I've learnt a lot during the process of developing this app. First and more important thing that I've learnt is the importance of testing, and the more the app was growing the more important the tests were. I've also learnt the importance of sketching and developing ideas on how the app should be. I've also learnt the importance of communication with a possible user to understand fully what the app should do.

Maker Pair

Coffee: 

/day

GitHub: maker-pair

This was a project that I worked in team with other fellow coders when I was still studying at Makers Academy. This project was meant to be used by new students who were starting the course at Makers Academy, by registering the student gets an access to all the students that had already finished the course and after consulting its criterias the new user can pair with the senior student for a pair programming session.

Approach

We first started by defining the MVP (minimum viable product) and after deciding that we started deciding the tasks for each pair, front-end and back-end. We rotated so all the people in the group could develop both sides of the project. The whole project was developed in two days by four people.

Conclusions

During the period developing this project I understood the importance of communication skills and listening skills when working in teams. I also learnt how important is to develop an MVP before starting working on a project, because when having an MVP is far easier to respect the deadline rather than getting lost on features.
